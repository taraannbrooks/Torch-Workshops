Our Workshops

The workshops that we hold are for all levels of learning, in order to use our studio you would need to take silversmithing 101 or pass a tools test due to safety in the studio. No experience is needed and all tools and materials are provided in the workshops. All you have to do is show up ready to learn! Each student will have their own set of hand tools and jewelry bench to work at. The goal is to learn and have fun!

Intro to Silversmithing

Let's get started with a bang! We will cover the basics of silversmithing from tools, how to use them to soldering.

Intro to Rings

Learn to make your very own ring, custom to fit you.

Intermediate Rings

Learn to use more advanced techniques with ring making, focus on pushing design and executing.
